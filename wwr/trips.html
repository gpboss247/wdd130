Featured Adventures

Los Dientes del Diablo

Expert

Brace yourself for the ultimate whitewater challenge. "The Deep River's Teeth" is our most intense run,a roaring stretch of Class V rapids carved through dramatic canyon walls. Only for experienced paddlers seeking the pinnacle of river adventure.

  • Difficulty: Class V
  • Duration: Full Day (8 hrs)
  • Group Size: 4-8 people
  • From $170 per person

Big Mallard

Intermediate

The Big Mallard is a classic whitewater run offering exciting Class III-IV rapids set against stunning mountain scenery. Perfect for those with some paddling experience looking to level up their river skills while still having a blast.

  • Difficulty: Class III-IV
  • Duration: Full Day (6 hrs)
  • Group Size: 4-10 people
  • From $115 per person

Half Day Adventure

Beginner

New to rafting? This is the perfect introduction! Our Half Day Adventure takes you through gentle Class I-II rapids with gorgeous river scenery. No experience necessary,our expert guides will have you paddling confidently in no time.

  • Difficulty: Class I-II
  • Duration: Half Day (3 hrs)
  • Group Size: 2-12 people
  • From $59 per person

River Canyon Expedition

Intermediate

Experience the grandeur of a spectacular river canyon on this multi-day journey. Camp under the stars, explore hidden side canyons, and navigate exciting Class III rapids through some of the most breathtaking scenery on earth.

  • Difficulty: Class III
  • Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights
  • Group Size: 6-12 people
  • From $329 per person

Wild Waters

Expert

Unleash the thrill seeker within on our Wild Waters run. Tackle a relentless series of Class IV-V rapids through untamed wilderness. This is raw, unfiltered river adventure for those who demand the absolute best from their outdoor experiences.

  • Difficulty: Class IV-V
  • Duration: Full Day (7 hrs)
  • Group Size: 4-8 people
  • From $159 per person
